Be alerted when Internet changes.

Sentinel watches any website for you on a daily basis and warns you if the content changes.

If it does, we'll take a screenshot, upload it to your Dropbox account and warn you by email.

Sentinel will launch at the end of 2013 to the first 500 people who subscribes. Then it will launch one month later for everybody else.

How will it work?

You sign up through Dropbox. We deposit a .json file in one of your Dropbox folder that you have to fill with the list of web pages you want to monitor. Each night, we check if the website has changed - if it does, the screenshot is placed into your Dropbox, and we'll warn you by email.

Who is it for?

This service is aimed at designers, marketers and product managers. The common use would be to take a close look at your competition. Another use would to be informed of changes on websites that you like and admire.

How much will it cost?

This service will have a small cost, but we are still trying to find the exact amount. We don't want to have to close two months after launching because our pricing was not realistic considering the number of servers needed for this service to run smoothly.
