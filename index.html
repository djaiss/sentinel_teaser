Be alerted when the Internet changes.

You tell Sentinel which sites are important to you. If any of them change, Sentinel will place a screenshot in your Dropbox and alert you by email.

Sentinel will launch some time in 2014. We're not in a hurry, because these things need to be done right rather than fast. We're also just two guys with jobs and kids. We're building Sentinel because we love it, not because we have to.

How does it work?

You sign up using your Dropbox account. A Sentinel folder will be created in your Dropbox in which we place a json file. This file contains a list of sites you may like, but feel free to add your own.

Once a day, we check the state of each of these sites and give you a screenshot in your DropBox if they change. We'll also send you an email, so you won't miss anything.

We won't see your Dropbox password, and we won't have access to anything outside the Sentinel folder in your Dropbox.

Who is it for?

Sentinel is made for designers, marketers and product managers who want to keep track of their competition. But anyone can use it to keep track of the sites they love.

How much will it cost?

Runing a service like this smoothly costs real money, so using it will have a small cost. Exact pricing will be established before launching. However, there will be a free trial, no credit card required, so you can see for yourself first.
